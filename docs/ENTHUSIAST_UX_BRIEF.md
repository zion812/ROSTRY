## üöÄ Key UX Interventions

### 1. The "Hero" Dashboard (Home)
Instead of a list of buttons, the home screen becomes a **Command Center**.
- **Transformation:** Replace standard KPI cards with a **"Farm Reputation Widget"** that glows based on Trust Score.
- **Action:** "Quick Actions" become a **Speed Dial** or a **Floating Dock** with haptic feedback.
- **Showcase:** Background of the home screen features a subtle slideshow of their top "Star Birds".

### 2. Gamified "Rooster Card" Creator üé¥
Creating a shareable card should feel like minting an NFT or printing a sports card.
- **Interaction:** Live preview with "Tilt" effect (parallax) using gyroscope.
- **Templates:** "Brawler", "Showstopper", "Geneticist" themes.
- **Effect:** Particle effects when a card is successfully generated.

### 3. "Arena" Discovery (Explore)
Browsing birds shouldn't look like a spreadsheet.
- **Layout:** Staggered Masonry grid for photos (Pinterest style) or Full-screen swipe (TikTok style) for "Showcase" clips.
- **Interaction:** Double-tap to "Respect" (Like) with a unique custom animation (e.g., a fist bump or crown icon).

### 4. Tactile Data Logging
- **Egg Collection:** Instead of a text input dialog, use a **Slider** or a **Stepper** with satisfying "click" sounds and haptics.
- **Incubation:** A visual circular progress ring that "fills up" as hatch day approaches.
