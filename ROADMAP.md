## Detailed Roadmap (2026)

### Q1 2026: Consolidation & Performance
#### 1. 3-Tier Domain Decoupling (ADR-004)
Scaling the data model to separate physical assets from commercial listings.
- **Success Criteria**: Zero data leakage between management/market domains; 40% reduction in ViewModel complexity.

#### 2. Performance Profiling
Optimizing app startup and canvas rendering (Digital Farm).
- **Success Criteria**: <2s cold start; stable 60fps during farm interaction.

### Q2 2026: Intelligence & Reach
#### 1. AI-Powered Insights
Disease prediction and growth optimization recommendations.
#### 2. Multi-Language Support
Expanding accessibility for rural farmers in regional languages.

### Q3-Q4 2026: Ecosystem Expansion
#### 1. IoT Sensor Integration
Real-time environmental monitoring for poultry sheds.
#### 2. Financial Services
Micro-insurance and credit scoring for active platform users.
